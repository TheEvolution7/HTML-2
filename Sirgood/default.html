10 THINGS SUCCESSFUL MOMPRENEURS DO

* April 3, 2021 *

When you take CBD, you may not notice the results you hoped for right away in some situations. In these situations, you must figure out why CBD may not be working, and once you know why you can adjust things to work better for you. 

Poor Quality CBD

Not all CBD products are the same. It is important to get to know what is in the CBD product you are using. In fact, studies have proven that some of the most well-known CBD brands have almost no CBD in them. 

To ensure that the product you purchase really has what the label says, you need to look at the COA – Certificate or Analysis. You should be able to find a CBD product’s COA on the company website. The COA is a third-party tester that will verify all the information that is contained on the label. It will also certify that the product doesn’t contain any dangerous substances, like heavy metals, pesticides, or molds. 

Getting the Right Amount of CBD

The amount of CBD you take per dose also matters. Some people base their dosage on what is recommended by friends or family members. The problem with this is that everyone is different. Everyone uniquely metabolizes natural products, regardless of if you are talking about hemp extract or caffeine. 

If the CBD products you are taking do not work well for you, it may be time to decrease or increase the amount you take. Your endocannabinoid system is responsible for regulating many types of physiological processes in your body, which include your nervous and immune systems, mood, digestion, and pain. 

It’s worth noting that the endocannabinoid system is not a one-size-fits-all. In fact, it varies from person to person. Because of this, it may be necessary to adjust your dosage to achieve the desired results. 

Unrealistic Expectations 

When you first start taking CBD, you may not have realistic expectations. They believe they will see results right away. For many people, this happens. However, for others, CBD takes much more time to start working. 

Don’t be discouraged or surprised if you don’t see significant results in the initial week of taking CBD. 

Interactions with Other Products You Take

It may be smart to space out when you take CBD and other medications. Sometimes, the CBD may interact with something else you are taking, which reduces the effects of the CBD. It is also highly recommended that you take CBD with foods full of healthy fats, such as avocado, yogurt, or peanut butter. Doing this will help ensure CBD is absorbed in your body, and it will be more effective when taken with fat. 

If you have started taking CBD but don’t see the desired results, consider some of the issues that may be causing this listed above. Sometimes slight adjustments in your routine will fix the issue and help you achieve the desired results with the CBD product you take. Being informed is the best way to take CBD and ensure it provides the desired results.
