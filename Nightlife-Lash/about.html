The Art of Lashes

Long Lasting

Made with the finest materials, Velour was created not only for comfort but also for longevity. Every pair of Velour lashes can be worn on average 25+ applications.

Natural & Lightweight

The use of fine mink and silk allows our products to mimic your natural lashes while providing you with volume and the most comfortable wear.

Ethically Sourced

Free from chemical processing and dyes, Velour lashes are hypoallergenic and sterilized so even the most sensitive eyes can wear them. Velour lashes are ethically sourced and are made using naturally shedding recycled mink hair.
